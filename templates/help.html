Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new camera to my system?

To add a new camera:

  1. Go to the Dashboard and click "Add Camera"
  2. Enter the camera's RTSP URL or IP address
  3. Configure the camera settings (name, zone, detection sensitivity)
  4. Test the connection and save the configuration

The camera will appear in your dashboard within a few seconds.

Why aren't my video feeds loading?

Common causes for video feed issues:

  • Network connectivity: Check your internet connection and camera network access
  • Camera offline: Verify the camera is powered on and connected
  • RTSP URL: Ensure the camera's RTSP stream URL is correct
  • Firewall: Make sure ports 554 (RTSP) and 1935 (RTMP) are open

Try running the troubleshooting tool or check the system logs for more details.

How do I configure motion detection zones?

To configure detection zones:

  1. Navigate to the Zones page from the sidebar
  2. Select a camera from the list
  3. Click "Edit Zones" to draw detection areas on the camera feed
  4. Use the drawing tools to define areas where motion should trigger alerts
  5. Save your zone configuration

You can create multiple zones per camera with different sensitivity levels.

How do I receive alerts on my mobile device?

To enable mobile alerts:

  1. Go to Settings > Notifications
  2. Enable "Push Notifications"
  3. Install the EyeQ mobile app on your device
  4. Log in with your account credentials
  5. Configure which types of alerts you want to receive

You'll receive instant notifications for motion detection, system alerts, and security events.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your email address associated with the account
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Follow the link to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
